Instructions

Imagine you are at a party. You walk into the kitchen and overhear some people talking.


In one part of the experiment, you'll read a conversation between two people. Here's an example:

Person 1: What about Cindy? Is she in New York City?
Person 2: She's in Los Angeles.

We'll ask you to decide whether Person 2 is certain that Cindy is in Los Angeles, given what he/she said.


In the other part of the experiment, you'll read a conversation between 3 people. For instance:

Person 1: What about Cindy? Is she in New York City?
Person 2: She's in Los Angeles.
Person 3: No, she's not in Los Angeles.

In this part of the experiment, we'll ask you to decide whether Person 3's response sounds natural in the conversation. You'll give your answers on a 7-point scale.

There are a total of 34 questions (17 in each part).

To make sure you are paying attention, we have included some questions for which we know the right answer. If you read the questions carefully, you will definitely get those right.

Payment: You will be paid $1 to participate in this experiment, which will take about 9 minutes to complete.

At the end of the experiment, we will ask you some short questions about you and your language background. You will be paid no matter what answers you give to those questions, so please answer truthfully.
